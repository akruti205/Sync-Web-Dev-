CORE IDEA

Sync is a platform that exists to provide a space to synchronize students, conversations and resources in one fluid experience.
Sync is built on a simple belief: learning and collaboration should be effortless.

In a world where students juggle endless tabs, scattered resources, and chaotic group chats, Sync brings it all together: one platform, one flow.
Whether you’re deep in self-study, brainstorming for a competition, or sharing resources from your domain, Sync keeps you connected, focused, and in rhythm with your peers.

GOALS

  • A single space for chatrooms, resources, and study tools.
  • Eliminating friction between tools, resources and like-minded students
  • Empower students to share knowledge across domains like finance, marketing, tech, and more
  • Create a culture where students help each other succeed, beyond their own campus.
  • Designing features that work just as well for a small group of friends as for a larger student community

FEATURES

  • Chatrooms:
    • Purpose: Domain-based chatrooms where students can instantly connect with like-minded peers
    • Database: The chatroom uses a firebase database to store and display messages.
    • Moderation: Currently, the chatrooms are not moderated and do not check for sensitive content, due to time and resource constraints. This feature will be implemented post this assignment.
    • Authentication: The chatrooms do not currently require a password login as we do not have a live database for users. This feature will be implemented post this assignment.
    • Multiple Chatrooms: Currently, there is one chatroom for all students. This feature will be modified to include various communities revolving around distinct domains such as management, finance, etc.

  • Pomodoro Timer:
    • Purpose: To help students to study in focussed time-bound session, using the Pomodoro technique of studying for 25 minutes and then taking a 5 minute break

  • Generate Link:
    • Purpose: To facilitate online group study sessions by sharing and joining meeting links. Links can be generated and then shared over chatrooms or with students' study partners.
    • Software: For this feature, we have integrated Jitsi link generators. Jitsi ia an open-source video conferencing software.

  • Study Partner Swipe and Match:
    • Purpose: Students can view each other's profiles and match with their perfect study partners to organise chatrooms and group study sessions with.
    • Functionality: Currently, the feature only has swipe functionality. Due to limited database and time constraints, we have not yet created further functionalities.

  • Resource Repository:
    • Purpose: To enable students to find and upload free online resources across various domains such as management, finance, CFA, case competitions, etc.
    • Moderation: Currently, the content is not moderated and do not check for sensitive content, due to time and resource constraints. This feature will be implemented post this assignment.

DESIGN RATIONALE

Typography & Font Choices

Our typography strategy emphasizes readability and modern appeal to resonate with Gen-Z users. We selected clean, sans-serif fonts that ensure optimal legibility across all devices and screen sizes. The font hierarchy creates clear information structure while maintaining the energetic, approachable vibe that appeals to our target demographic. Consistent font weights and sizes throughout the platform establish visual coherence and professional credibility.

Color Palette & Visual Identity

The vibrant color scheme combines dark backgrounds with bright accent colors to create a contemporary, engaging aesthetic that aligns with Gen-Z preferences for bold, high-contrast interfaces. The strategic use of red and pink gradients against dark backgrounds not only creates visual impact but also reduces eye strain during extended study sessions. This palette choice reflects the "Where Brains Meet Vibes" concept by balancing serious academic functionality with an energetic, social atmosphere.

Layout & User Experience Design

Our responsive design prioritizes intuitive navigation and seamless user flow. The layout employs modern web standards with flexible grid systems that adapt beautifully across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Strategic use of whitespace and component spacing creates breathing room that prevents cognitive overload while studying. The interface design follows contemporary UI/UX principles, incorporating familiar interaction patterns that reduce the learning curve for new users.
